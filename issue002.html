FROM THE ARTICLE

Imagine a classroom where every student has a private tutor, available at any hour, ready to explain a difficult idea in a dozen different ways. For a growing number of learners, this is no longer science fiction. Artificial intelligence has entered education, and it is changing how people study, write, and even think.
