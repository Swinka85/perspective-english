FROM THE ARTICLE

In recent years, Barcelona has seen something strange. Some local residents have walked through crowded tourist districts carrying water pistols, spraying visitors as a form of protest. It was not meant as a joke. For many people who live there, tourism has become part of a bigger question: who really owns a city?
